A place to collect and organize my notes

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}} Molecular Dynamics is a Hugo site using the Docsy theme. As I started designing a new course, *Practical Molecular Dynamics*, I found myself collecting an increasing amount of tutorials, code, and references, and needed a convenient way to organize them. I previously built a website using Hugo for organizing my PyMOL notes, and liked the way content was organized in documentation themes. {{% /blocks/lead %}}
